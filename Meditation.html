Tips for Beginners

Start Small: Begin with just a few minutes each day to build a habit.

Find a Quiet Space: Choose a place where you won't be disturbed during your meditation.

Focus on Your Breath: Use your breath as an anchor to bring your attention back when your mind wanders.

Don't Judge Yourself: It's normal for your mind to wander. Gently bring your focus back without judgment.
